Mission

Sacred Connections Ministry is a non-profit organization that is committed to our congregation living in their highest selves. We strive to achieve this elevated spiritual self through guided meditation, professional workshops, visualization and the healing powers of a spiritual sacrament, cannabis. Through this holy work, an individual can experience alignment on a conscious, subconscious and super conscious level which can foster actualization of this higher self.

Meditation

Meditation cultivates a deeper understanding of one's inner self, patterns of behavior, and emotional responses, fostering clarity and personal insight.

Workshops

Workshops include courses in self-development that foster a deeper understanding of ourselves to move beyond our own limitations.

Visualization

By allowing individuals to mentally picture their goals, emotions, or ideal self can make the abstract concepts more tangible and easier to integrate.

Sacrament

The use of the spiritual sacrament of cannabis promotes alignment physically, mentally, and spiritually for those who seek it.

Our Philosophy

A Higher Connection

We believe that people are most effective in their lives with a deep level of awareness. To attain this higher connection, one must be free of anger and pain to live within this vibration.

Spiritual Sacrament

The spiritual Sacrament of Cannabis aids and supports the attainment of a higher vibration through its healing properties as an herbal entheogen in its various forms.

Various Forms of Sacrament

Holy anointing oil, topical salves, and spiritual salts for bathing used in tandem with the ancient healing powers of crystals, chakra-based artwork and sacred geometry promote physical, emotional, and spiritual well-being and self-awareness.

Awareness

Self-awareness grants us the ability to develop ourselves so we are better equipped to work with and serve others.

Goals

Our goal is to serve our congregation and inspire them to form sacred connections with self and others.
